Turkey Burgers

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
ground turkey 1 pound
jalepenos 1 cup
buns 2 pairs

How to make: Mix and stir ground turkey with jalepenos. Cook for 25 min on medium temp. Slap on buns.

Lasagna Flatbread

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
ricotta cheese 15 oz
mozzarella cheese 1 cup
tomato sauce 1 cup
flatbread 2 slices

How to make: Spread tomato sauce on flat bread. Bake for 10 minutes. Sprinkle and spread cheeses on top. Bake for another 3 minutes.

Mayo Sandwhich

  • Time: 2 min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
bread 2 slices
mayo 1 cup

How to make: Slap all the mayo on the bread and clamp.
